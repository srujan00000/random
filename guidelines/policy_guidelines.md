# Policy Guidelines

## Prohibited Content
- Violence, weapons, harmful activities
- Discrimination (race, gender, religion)
- Misleading claims or false information
- Copyrighted materials or trademarked logos
- Political or controversial content
- Adult or explicit content

## Brand Voice
- Professional and credible tone
- Inclusive, gender-neutral language
- No exaggerated claims
- Positive messaging (avoid fear-based)

## Platform Rules
| Platform | Tone | Hashtags |
|----------|------|----------|
| LinkedIn | Professional | Max 5 |
| Instagram | Casual, emojis OK | Max 30 |
| Facebook | Conversational | Max 3 |

## Legal
- Label sponsored content
- Disclose AI-generated content when required
- Use only licensed/original content
