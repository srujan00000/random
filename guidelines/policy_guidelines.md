# Brand Policy Guidelines

## Overview
These guidelines ensure all AI-generated content aligns with company brand standards and legal requirements.

---

## 1. Content Restrictions

### Prohibited Content
- Violence, weapons, or harmful activities
- Discriminatory content (race, gender, religion, disability)
- Misleading claims or false information
- Copyrighted materials or trademarked logos
- Political statements or controversial opinions
- Adult or explicit content

### Sensitive Topics (Require Approval)
- Health and medical claims
- Financial advice
- Legal matters
- Environmental claims

---

## 2. Brand Voice

### Tone Guidelines
- **Professional**: Avoid slang, maintain credibility
- **Inclusive**: Use gender-neutral language
- **Authentic**: No exaggerated claims
- **Positive**: Focus on benefits, avoid fear-based messaging

### Language Rules
- Use active voice
- Avoid jargon unless industry-specific
- Keep sentences concise (max 25 words)
- No ALL CAPS except acronyms

---

## 3. Platform-Specific Rules

### LinkedIn
- Professional tone required
- No emojis in first line
- Max 5 hashtags

### Instagram
- Casual tone acceptable
- Emojis encouraged
- Max 30 hashtags

### Twitter/X
- Concise messaging
- Max 2 hashtags
- Avoid controversial topics

---

## 4. Legal Compliance

### Required Disclosures
- Sponsored content must be labeled
- AI-generated content should be disclosed when required
- Contest/giveaway rules must be included

### Copyright
- Only use licensed or original content
- Credit sources when applicable
- Avoid mimicking competitor branding

---

## Checklist for Review

- [ ] No prohibited content
- [ ] Appropriate tone for platform
- [ ] Legal disclosures included
- [ ] Brand voice consistent
- [ ] No copyright violations
