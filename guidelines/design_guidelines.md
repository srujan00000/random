# Design Guidelines

## Colors
- Use professional, brand-appropriate colors
- High contrast for text overlays
- Avoid neon/overly saturated colors

## Composition
- Subject centered or rule-of-thirds
- Clean backgrounds for product shots
- Proper lighting, no harsh shadows

## Quality
- Minimum 1080p resolution
- Sharp focus on main subject
- No watermarks or stock artifacts

## Video Specific
- Hook within first 3 seconds
- Smooth transitions
- No excessive camera shake

## Accessibility
- Sufficient color contrast
- Avoid flashing/strobing effects
