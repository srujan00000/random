# Brand Design Guidelines

## Overview
Visual standards for AI-generated images and videos to maintain brand consistency.

---

## 1. Color Palette

### Primary Colors
- Use brand-approved colors only
- Maintain sufficient contrast (WCAG AA minimum)
- Avoid neon or overly saturated colors in professional content

### Color Usage
- Background: Light/neutral tones preferred
- Text overlays: High contrast required
- Accent colors: Use sparingly for CTAs

---

## 2. Typography (When Applicable)

### Font Guidelines
- Sans-serif fonts for digital content
- Minimum readable size for social media
- No decorative fonts for body text

### Text Placement
- Rule of thirds for text positioning
- Avoid text over busy backgrounds
- Leave padding around text edges

---

## 3. Image Composition

### Framing
- Subject centered or rule-of-thirds
- Clean backgrounds for product shots
- Appropriate negative space

### Quality Standards
- High resolution (min 1080p)
- Proper lighting (avoid harsh shadows)
- Sharp focus on main subject

### Prohibited Elements
- Watermarks or stock photo artifacts
- Distorted or stretched images
- Pixelated or low-quality content

---

## 4. Video Standards

### Technical Requirements
- Minimum 1080p resolution
- Stable footage (no excessive shake)
- Clear audio if applicable

### Pacing
- Hook within first 3 seconds
- Appropriate length per platform:
  - TikTok/Reels: 15-60 seconds
  - LinkedIn: 30-120 seconds
  - YouTube: Platform-dependent

### Transitions
- Smooth, professional transitions
- Avoid flashy/distracting effects
- Consistent style throughout

---

## 5. Accessibility

### Requirements
- Sufficient color contrast
- Alternative text descriptions for images
- Captions for videos
- Avoid flashing/strobing effects

---

## Checklist for Review

- [ ] Brand colors used correctly
- [ ] Proper image composition
- [ ] Appropriate resolution
- [ ] Accessible design
- [ ] No prohibited visual elements
- [ ] Consistent styling
